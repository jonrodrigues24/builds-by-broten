About Jens & Co

Builds by Broten is a Custom Furniture Business based out of Albuquerque, New Mexico. We create unique, one of a kind furniture that will make any room stand out. Each piece is thoughtfully designed and built by owner Jens Broten, combining his passions for welding and wood working to produce quality, custom furniture.
